Welcome to Sagar Foundation

(Origin of life)


Welcome to Social Action Group for Awareness and Research (SAGAR) Foundation.
SAGAR Foundation is a Government approved Registered Trust (Redg. No E/21046/Ahmedabad) working on a philosophy of “Social Service with Professional Approach”.
Vision: To build self-sustained social service organization working towards bridging the gap between social need and social action through research.
Mission: To analyze social need in scientific manners, to identify them and support the society through amalgamation of professional services with social action.
