About Sagar Foundation

(Origin of life)

SAGAR Foundation is a Government approved Registered Trust (Redg. No E/21046/Ahmedabad) working on a philosophy of “Social Service with Professional Approach”. With the vision to build self-sustained social service organization working towards bridging the gap between social need and social action through research, trust is working towards analysing social need in scientific manners, to identify them and support the society through amalgamation of professional services with social action.

SAGAR Foundation is committed towards the upliftment of Bottom of Pyramid through is wide ranging activities from counselling to environment. SAGAR Foundation is engaged in training in the area of research, data analysis, research publication and many other professional and development services as part of its fund-raising activities. Fund generated through professional services is utilized for social services.

Vision
