As reported by National Academy of RUDSETI, India has a glorious record of entrepreneurial achievements. Tremendous growth of Indian Economy during the last decade has been driven by the entrepreneurs, who have explored the potential of the country and unleashed their entrepreneurship to create wealth for the country. Most of these achievers are first generation entrepreneurs, who hail from non entrepreneurial back ground. Experiments & researches have proved that it is possible to develop entrepreneurship by motivation, proper training & facilitation.

Proposed Self-employment training programs will include:

  • Entrepreneurial training
  • Business counseling
  • Technical assistance
  • Requirements to engage in activities relating to setting up a business and becoming self-employed.
  • Management Training
  • Market need assessment training
